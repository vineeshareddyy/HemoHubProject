O+ blood can be given to any Rh-positive patient (O+, A+, B+, AB+). O+ individuals can receive blood from O+ and O- donors.

A+ blood can be given to A+ and AB+ individuals. A+ individuals can receive blood from A+, A-, O+, and O- donors.

B+ blood can be given to B+ and AB+ individuals. B+ individuals can receive blood from B+, B-, O+, and O- donors.

As the universal recipient, AB+ individuals can receive blood from any blood type (AB+, AB-, A+, A-, B+, B-, O+, O-), but can only donate to other AB+ individuals.

As the universal donor, O- blood can be given to patients of any blood type (O-, O+, A-, A+, B-, B+, AB-, AB+). However, O- individuals can only receive blood from other O- donors..

A- blood can be given to A- and A+ as well as AB- and AB+ individuals. A- individuals can receive blood from A- and O- donors.

B- blood can be given to B- and B+ as well as AB- and AB+ individuals. B- individuals can receive blood from B- and O- donors..

AB- blood can be given to AB- and AB+ individuals. AB- individuals can receive blood from AB-, A-, B-, and O- donors.
